THE ELECTRIC LIGHTING ACT makes it mandatory to use the services of a Licensed Electrician
      Customers must ensure licensed electricians present a valid electrical license and a notice form
      Customers should demand a receipt from all licensed electricians
      All Licensed Electricians must provide business address and phone number
      Jobs of 70amps and over should be done and submitted by a licensed electrician
      Electrical services/certification charge should only be paid to a licensed electrician
      All electrical work on solar installations must be done by a licensed electrician
      Customer must ensure they know the licensed electrician whose name is on their REPORT
